Joshua Josef Mort and his team at WebsiteAnimation.co.uk are dedicated to crafting visually stunning websites and animations. Based in South Wales, we cater to both local businesses and clients worldwide, combining a personal touch with professional excellence.

After Effects

Adobe After Effects elevates your projects with professional-grade motion graphics, visual effects, and animations. It is used to create interactive 2D animations for websites and stunning video editing animations, adding dynamic flair and captivating visuals to your content.

Blender

Blender enables us to create stunning 3D character customizations, detailed models, and smooth animations, bringing your vision to life with engaging, high-quality 3D elements for your website or video content.

Webflow

Webflow offers a powerful platform for building responsive, visually stunning websites without the need for coding. It provides customizable design tools and seamless CMS integration, allowing you to create interactive web experiences that are both beautiful and functional.

CSS/Java

CSS shapes the visual design and layout of your site for a cohesive look across all devices, while JavaScript enhances user interaction and functionality, making your website dynamic and responsive to user actions.
